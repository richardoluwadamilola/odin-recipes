One-Pot Spagetti

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Black pepper
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Diced Tomato
  • water
  • Chilli powder
  • Dried Oregano
  • Spagetti
  • Permesan Cheese(Optional)

Steps

  1. Heat a dutch oven over medium-high heat. Cook and stir ground beef, onion and garlic until beef is browned and crumbly and onion is translucent, 5 t0 7 minutes. Drain and discard excess grease.
  2. Stir in tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, water, chilli powder, oregano, and blck pepper. Bring to a boil, cover, reduce heat and let simmer, stirring often for about, 30 minutes.
  3. Add Spagetti to pot, cover and simmer, stirring often, until pasta is tender, about 20 minutes. Served topped with permesan cheese.
