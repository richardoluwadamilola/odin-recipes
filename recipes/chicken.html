Amazing Crusted Chicken

Ingredients

  • Cheese flavored crackers
  • Onions
  • Sesame seeds
  • Black pepper
  • Boneless chicken
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450degrees F(230 degrees C).
  2. Mix cheese-flavored crackers, onions, bread crumbs, sesame seed, salt and pepper in a bowl and set aside.
  3. Wash and pat chicken to dry, spread a thin layer of mayonnaise on one side of each piece and place mayonnaise-side down in the crackers mixture, do same for the other side.
  4. Bake in preheated oven until chicken breasts is no longer pink in the middle.
