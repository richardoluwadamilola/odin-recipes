Chocolate Cookies

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • White Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Baking Soda
  • Hot water
  • Salt
  • All-purpose flour
  • Semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C).
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking powder in hot water, add to batter along with salt, Stir in the flour, chocolate chips and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  3. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
